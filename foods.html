Foods

These are the foods that the traditional african communities used to eat and some of the communities still eat them.Different communities ate different foods and some communities did not eat food from others because there were taboos against that.

South Africa

The following are the traditional foods in South Africa

  1. Biltong

    This meat that is cured with vinegar and salt and hung out in free air to dry.Its a traditional snack.

  2. Boerewors

    This is a traditional South African sausage made from beef, mixed with either pork or lamb and a mixture of spices.

    Other foods are fufu,bani chow etc.

East Africa

Traditional foods in EAst Africa

  1. Cassava

    2. This is a root vegetable and can be milled to have flour.

  2. Sea Foods

    3. This is any sea life considered as human food.
    Many of traditional east african communities ate sea foods apart from the cushites which had a taboo against eating fish.
